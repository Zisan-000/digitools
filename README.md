# 🚀 Digitools

**Digitools** is a premium marketplace designed to supercharge digital workflows. It provides a seamless experience for creators and professionals to browse, manage, and purchase high-end AI-powered tools and design assets.

---

## live Link:

(https://digitools-system.netlify.app/)

## ✨ Features

- **🛒 Interactive Shopping Experience:** Browse a curated list of digital products with real-time status updates (e.g., "Added to Cart").
- **❌ Smart Cart Management:** Easily review your selections and remove items dynamically with instant subtotal and total recalculations.
- **💳 Seamless Checkout Flow:** A dedicated "Proceed to Payment" workflow that clears your session and provides instant feedback upon successful transaction.

## 📝 Usage

- **Adding Items:** Click the "Buy Now" button on any product card. The button will update to "Added to Cart" to prevent duplicates.
- **Removing Items:** Click the "Cart" button in the navbar, then click the "Remove" link next to any item in your list.
- **Checkout:** Click "Proceed to Checkout" to clear your cart and receive a success notification via Toastify.
